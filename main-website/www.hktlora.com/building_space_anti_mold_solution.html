Technical Features

Advanced Technology for Comprehensive Protection

01

High-Precision Temperature Monitoring

Our sensors provide ±0.5°C temperature accuracy with real-time data transmission every 30 seconds, ensuring immediate detection of temperature fluctuations that could lead to mold growth.

02

Precision Humidity Control

With ±2%RH humidity accuracy, our system monitors relative humidity levels continuously, alerting you when humidity exceeds 60% - the threshold where mold begins to grow.

03

Multi-Level Alert System

Customizable alert levels allow you to set different thresholds for warning, caution, and critical conditions. Receive alerts via SMS, email, or push notifications.

04

Historical Data Analysis

Access up to 2 years of historical data to identify patterns, analyze trends, and make data-driven decisions about ventilation and humidity control.

05

Cloud-Based Dashboard

Monitor all your building spaces from anywhere with our intuitive cloud dashboard. View real-time data, historical trends, and sensor status on any device.

06

Easy Installation & Maintenance

Wireless sensors with battery backup for up to 2 years. Quick setup in under 30 minutes with no complex wiring required. Calibration-free operation.
